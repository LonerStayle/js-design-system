  • Light — bright, indirect. Keep out of harsh midday sun, which scorches the fine fronds.
  • Water — keep the soil evenly moist, never soggy. Let only the very top dry between drinks.
  • Humidity — loves 50%+. Group with other plants or mist on dry days; thrives near a kitchen sink.
  • Feed — half-strength balanced fertiliser monthly through spring and summer.
