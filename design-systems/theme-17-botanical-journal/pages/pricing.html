Questions, pressed flat

Everything about billing, your data, and your team — answered plainly.

Annual billing charges once a year and works out about 20% cheaper — roughly two months free. A Gardener plan is $12/month or $115/year. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time and we'll prorate the difference onto your next invoice.
Yes. Cancel from Settings → Billing in two clicks — no email required, no retention maze. You keep paid features until the end of the current period, then drop to the free Seedling tier. Your specimens are never deleted; drawers beyond the free limit are simply locked, not erased.
Always. Your pressings, notes, and photos are yours. Gardener exports your whole herbarium to PDF and CSV in one tap, and Curator adds standards-based Darwin Core archives and direct GBIF publishing — so your records stay portable to any institution.
Curator includes up to 8 named seats at no extra cost. Invite co-curators by email, assign roles (Viewer, Editor, or Lead), and share specific catalogues rather than your whole library. Need more than 8 seats for a university herbarium or studio? Reach out and we'll tailor a collection plan.
The Seedling plan is free forever with no card. Paid tiers come with a 14-day trial that doesn't ask for payment up front — press, scan, and export to your heart's content, and only add a card if you decide to keep growing.
