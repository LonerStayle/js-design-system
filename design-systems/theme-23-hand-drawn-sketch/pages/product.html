We made the notebook we always wanted on our own desks. The spine is sewn — not glued — so it falls open and stays open while both your hands are busy sketching. No more pinning the page down with a coffee mug.

Inside, 192 pages of warm cream stock take fountain ink, felt-tip, and pencil without a hint of bleed-through. The back of the book hides a gusseted pocket for loose scraps, tickets, and the receipts you keep meaning to file. Every sketchbook ships with our signature felt-tip pen tucked under the elastic.

Hand-assembled in small batches. No two ship exactly the same — that's the point.
