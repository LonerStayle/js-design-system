Questions, answered

Everything about billing, plan changes, and getting your studio set up.

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade any time from your studio settings. When you upgrade, the change takes effect immediately and we prorate the difference for the rest of your billing period. Downgrades apply at the start of your next cycle so you keep everything you've paid for.
When you pay for a full year up front, you get two months free — that's the 20% saving shown on the toggle above. Studio drops to $23/mo ($276/yr) and Workshop to $63/mo ($756/yr). You can switch from monthly to yearly at any time and we'll credit your remaining balance.
We'll never freeze your shop mid-sale. If you reach your monthly order cap, orders keep flowing and we'll send a friendly nudge to upgrade. Hobbyist studios that consistently exceed the free tier are invited to move to Studio — no surprise overage fees, ever.
Yes — there are no contracts and no cancellation fees. Cancel from your billing page and you'll keep full access until the end of the period you've already paid for. Your products, orders, and firing history stay exported-ready, so you can take your data with you.
We love community kilns and ceramics schools. Registered educational and non-profit studios get 30% off Workshop. Reach out to our team with a few details about your space and we'll get you set up with a custom plan.
