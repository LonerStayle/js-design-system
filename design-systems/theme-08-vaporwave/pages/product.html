The AURORA-88 is built for late-night sessions on the boardwalk of your mind. Two analog oscillators per voice drift and detune just enough to feel alive, then pour through a self-oscillating 24dB ladder filter that sings when you push it.

A glossy aluminium top plate catches every neon reflection, and the full-size semi-weighted keybed has the snap you want for stabs and the give you want for pads. Patch points line the back so it plays nice with your modular rack, your tape machine, and that dusty drum computer you refuse to sell.

  • 8 voices of true analog polyphony
  • Built-in tape chorus, spring-style reverb and BBD delay
  • 64-step sequencer with per-step probability and ratchets
  • USB-C, MIDI DIN, CV/Gate and audio over USB
