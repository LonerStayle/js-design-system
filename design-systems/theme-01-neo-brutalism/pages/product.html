Built to be hammered

The Runner OG is the trainer we kept asking other brands to make and they never did, so we built it ourselves. A flat 8mm-drop foam slab keeps you connected to the ground. The structured TPU cage is heat-bonded — no glue blobs, no peeling — and the heel counter is rigid enough to lock your stride without breaking your ankles.

Up top, a single-piece engineered mesh breathes through summer and the reflective 3M heel tab keeps you visible on 6am runs. Recycled rubber lugs bite wet pavement and shrug off curbs. It is not delicate. That is the point.
