Biography

Mara has spent a decade in the weeds of developer infrastructure — first at a fintech where CI took 40 minutes, then at an observability startup, and now full-time on open source. She believes the fastest test is the one you never have to run, which is why caching and incremental computation are her north star.

Off the clock she repairs mechanical keyboards, runs a small zine about brutalist web design, and is slowly translating the Spindle docs into Portuguese. She mentors two engineers through the Outreachy program and answers far too many GitHub issues.

Availability: open to advisory work and conference talks for Q3.
