02—Billing questions

Both bill per seat. Annual is paid once for the year and works out to two months free — a 20% saving versus paying month to month. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time; the change applies on your next renewal.

A seat is one person with edit access. Viewers are always free and unlimited. You can add seats anytime — we prorate the cost for the rest of your cycle. Removing a seat takes effect at the next renewal, so you're never charged twice for the same person.

Yes. Cancel from Settings → Billing and your workspace stays active until the end of the paid period — no cancellation fee. After that it converts to a read-only archive so your tokens and components are never lost.

All major credit and debit cards, plus SEPA direct debit for EUR accounts. Enterprise customers can pay annually by invoice and bank transfer with NET-30 terms. Every charge generates a downloadable VAT receipt.

We offer 50% off Studio for verified students, educators and registered non-profits, and the Starter plan is free for open-source projects. Reach out from the contact form with proof of eligibility and we'll apply the discount within a day.
