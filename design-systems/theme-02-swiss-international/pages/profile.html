01—Biography

Elena Brandt is a senior designer at RASTER Studio in Zürich, where she leads the studio's wayfinding and editorial practice. Trained at the Zürcher Hochschule der Künste, she spent her early years setting type by hand before moving to systematic, grid-driven digital work.

Her work is rooted in the International Typographic Style: objective, legible, and unsentimental. She believes hierarchy belongs to type and the grid — not to colour, shadow, or ornament — and that a single, disciplined accent does more than a full palette ever could.

Outside the studio she teaches a yearly workshop on baseline grids, maintains an open-source hairline chart kit, and keeps a stubborn allegiance to exactly one shade of red.
