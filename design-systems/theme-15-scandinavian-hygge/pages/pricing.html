🍵 Questions

Frequently asked

Still wondering about something? Brew a cup and read on.

Monthly plans are charged each month and you can leave any time. Yearly plans are billed once up front and save you 20% — that's roughly two and a half months free. Switching from monthly to yearly applies the discount immediately and we prorate the difference.
Always. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your workspace settings in a couple of clicks — no calls, no retention maze. If you downgrade, the new limits apply at the start of your next cycle, so nothing disappears mid-month.
Yes. Yearly plans come with a 30-day, no-questions refund if Hygge isn't the cosy fit you hoped for. For monthly plans, cancel before your next renewal and you won't be charged again. Reach out at hej@hygge.studio and a real human will sort it.
A seat is any member who can create or edit work. Cabin and Homestead are priced per member, and you only pay for active seats — deactivate someone and the seat frees up at the next cycle. Guests and clients who only view or approve work don't take up a paid seat.
Your work is yours. Every plan — including free Seedling — can export projects, tasks, files, and client records to CSV or JSON at any time. There's no lock-in and no export fee. If you ever pause your account, we keep your data safe and read-only for 90 days.

Still curious? Write to us at hej@hygge.studio — we reply within a day.
