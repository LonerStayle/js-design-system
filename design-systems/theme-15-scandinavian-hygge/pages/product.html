Quiet craft for cosy corners

The Fjord began as a sketch on a workshop chalkboard — a low, round table that could slip beside an armchair without crowding the room. We kept the silhouette honest: a softly bevelled top, three tapered legs splayed for stability, and a hand-rubbed oil finish that lets the oak breathe and warm over the years.

It pairs beautifully with a linen throw and a ceramic mug, and it is small enough to wander — from the sofa today to the bedside tomorrow. Like all Nordic Hearth pieces, it is built to be lived with, not looked at.

Everyday

Wipe with a soft, slightly damp cloth and dry straight away. Skip harsh sprays — warm water and a little patience are all the oak asks for.

Re-oil twice a year

Rub a thin coat of the supplied hardwax oil along the grain, leave 20 minutes, then buff. It keeps the surface water-resistant and lovely to touch.

Mind the sun

Oak mellows with light. Rotate the table now and then so it tans evenly, and keep it away from radiators to avoid drying the wood.
